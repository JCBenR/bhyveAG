Simplify Your Life

long range

wifi

The base station at your home, shop or office connects to the Internet--then broadcasts the Internet for miles to your pivots.

one touch

mapping

Setting up stops, VRI and End Gun controls has never been easier--just tap on the map of your pivot.

detection
